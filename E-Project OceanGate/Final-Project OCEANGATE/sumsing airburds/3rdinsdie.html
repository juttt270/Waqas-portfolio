Samsung Galaxy Buds Live

Model Number: SM-R180

(130 Reviews)

The Galaxy Buds Live offer a unique design combined with premium sound quality. Featuring Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) and a comfortable fit, these earbuds deliver an immersive audio experience without sacrificing style.

$169.99

Product Description

Immersive Sound
Active Noise Cancellation
Open-Ear Comfort

The Galaxy Buds Live deliver deep, rich bass and crisp highs with 12mm speakers tuned by AKG, ensuring an immersive sound experience for music, movies, and calls.

With Active Noise Cancellation (ANC), these earbuds block unwanted noise, helping you enjoy your media without distractions. ANC is ideal for noisy environments like cafes or public transport.

The open-ear design ensures a comfortable fit without putting pressure on your ear canal, making them perfect for all-day use, whether at work, the gym, or traveling.

Key Benefits:

  • Unique bean-shaped design for a comfortable and natural fit.
  • Active Noise Cancellation blocks distractions for a focused listening experience.
  • Open-ear design allows for comfort during extended wear without discomfort.
  • Long battery life with up to 8 hours of playback and 21 hours with the charging case.
  • IPX2 water resistance for protection against sweat and light rain.
  • Three microphones for clear voice calls even in noisy environments.
